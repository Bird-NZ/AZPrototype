Retail ops team workshop notes

- Store managers want one place to check daily performance
- Top questions:
  - What stores are below forecast today?
  - Which products are out of stock most often?
  - What issues are blocking operations this week?
- Prototype does NOT need ERP writeback
- Needs login path in the design, even if auth is lightweight in v1
- Monitoring and audit trail matter because internal users will demo this to leadership
- Prefer a web app plus simple API foundation for future integrations
