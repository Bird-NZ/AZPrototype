# Retail Insights Assistant — Executive Brief

## Goal
Create an internal Azure-based prototype that helps retail managers understand sales, stock, and store operations through a simple web interface and AI assistant.

## Success criteria
- Managers can ask natural-language questions about sales and stock
- Staff can view a dashboard of store KPIs
- Architecture is secure and credible for enterprise follow-on work
- Prototype can be deployed quickly and demonstrated safely

## Constraints
- Prototype budget should stay modest
- Prefer Azure-native services
- Keep the first version simple enough for a short demo

## Desired outputs
- Azure architecture
- app scaffold
- infrastructure definition
- deployment approach
- backlog for production hardening
